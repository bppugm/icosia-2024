Editorial policy

Focus and scope

The 5th International Conference on Smart and Innovative Agriculture (ICoSIA 2024) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies and directions in agriculture.

This year, the conference will take the theme “Feeding the Planet Sustainably: a Convergence of Technology, Ecology, and Community” with seven symposia: Agricultural Big Data Analysis symposium; Agricultural Geography symposium; Land and Environmental Management symposium; Precision Nutrition Technology symposium; Smart and Precision Farming symposium; Smart Genetics Resource Management and Utilization symposium; and Sustainable Food Production symposium.

ICoSIA UGM 2024 is in conjunction with the 5th Geoscience and Environmental Management Symposium and the 10th International Conference on Science and Technology.

Peer-review process

The criteria employed for the evaluation of submissions encompass several key aspects: relevance and timeliness, technical content and scientific rigour, novelty and originality, quality of presentation, and overall recommendation. Reviewers are required to provide detailed comments that justify their recommendations, including suggestions for enhancements in both technical content and presentation quality. Additionally, they are expected to submit confidential remarks directed to the Technical Program Committee (TPC).

An email notification requesting the review will be dispatched to the reviewer via the EDAS conference management system. Reviewers will have access to a list of assigned papers along with the abstracts of each submission. They may select "accept review" if the paper aligns with their area of expertise or "decline" if it falls outside their domain. Upon selecting "accept review," reviewers will receive comprehensive instructions for conducting the review, which will include specific criteria for scoring.

The ultimate decision regarding the acceptance or rejection of papers will be made during the TPC meeting, chaired by the TPC chair. This decision will be based on a thorough analysis of the review results, review scores, and plagiarism assessments.

Review process:

  1. Author submits the manuscript;
  2. Editor evaluation (some manuscripts are rejected or returned before the review process);
  3. Similarity check;
  4. Blind peer review process;
  5. Editor decision; and
  6. Notification to the authors.

Attending in the conference

After your final manuscript and presentation document has been submitted, you also need to attend the conference for a live Q&A session. Please note that you need to make time on October 23–24 in the Yogyakarta, Indonesia, because the conference program cannot be rescheduled and must be in accordance with the program given by the committee.

If you are unable to attend for any reason, make sure that one of the co-authors can replace your presence. Change of attendance will result in a change of name listed in the certificate which we will send by email. Unpresented papers will be removed from publication and automatically declared as rejected.
