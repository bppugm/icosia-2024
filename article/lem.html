    Publications

    We are delighted to share that a selection of outstanding papers—accepted and presented during the conference—has been published in the proceedings of BIO Web of Conferences. These proceedings are indexed in prominent international databases, including Scopus, Conference Proceedings Citation Index (Web of Science) (subject to acceptance, as with all proceedings journals), and the Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ). This ensures that the valuable research contributions from our community receive broad visibility and are accessible to scholars worldwide.

       

    We extend our sincere gratitude to all authors, reviewers, and participants for their dedication and engagement, which have been instrumental in achieving this milestone.
